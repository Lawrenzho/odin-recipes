Pasta Sauce with Italian Sausage

A flavorful homemade recipe for those who are tired of sweet pasta sauces.

Incredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Meat

Steps

  1. First, boil the pasta in hot water
  2. Then drain the pasta from the water when it is ready
  3. Next slice tomatoes and onions
  4. After that, fry the tomatoes and onions in hot oil
  5. Grind garlic and pepper
  6. Finally add the pasta to the ingredients and stir
